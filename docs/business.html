AI for Small Business Kit 🔧

From @techyai_cj. Seven prompts that save real hours for real businesses: trades, shops, services, anyone. Works in Claude or ChatGPT. Tap copy, swap the brackets, send the results.

01 · the flyer

Make me a flyer image for [offer, like a summer AC tune up special, $89]. Bold headline, easy to read from a car, include [phone number or business name]. Then make the price twice as big.

02 · the estimate

Draft a professional estimate for this job: [describe the job]. My rates: [labor rate, typical materials]. Line items, subtotal, simple terms, friendly closing. Make it look like it came from an established company.

03 · the follow up machine

Write a friendly check in text for customers from last month asking how everything is running, mentioning I appreciate referrals. Give me 3 versions: casual, professional, short.

04 · the invoice audit

Here are my supplier invoices from this month. Which prices went up compared to earlier invoices, by how much, and what is that costing me per year? [paste or attach invoices]

05 · the review reply

Reply to this customer review in my voice: professional, human, no corporate speak. If it is negative, acknowledge the issue, do not argue, and take it offline. [paste review]

06 · the no show killer

Write my appointment reminder messages: one for 24 hours before, one for 2 hours before. Friendly, short, with an easy way to reschedule. My business: [type].

07 · the weekly numbers

Here are my jobs and payments from this week. Tell me: total revenue, my best paying type of work, anything unpaid I should chase, and one thing I should do differently next week. [paste]
